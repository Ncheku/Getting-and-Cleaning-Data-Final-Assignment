Getting and Cleaning Data - Final Assignment Code Book

There are three types of variables in the file tidyDS.txt.

1 - Subject - an ID that represents the subject that perform the tests. It goes from 1 to 30.

2 - Activity - the activities performed when the measurements were collected.

	1 - walking: subject was walking during the test
	2 - walking_upstairs: subject was walking up a staircase during the test
	3 - walkings_downstairs: subject was walking down a staircase during the test
	4 - sitting: subject was sitting during the test
	5 - standing: subject was standing during the test
	6 - laying: subject was laying down during the test

3 - Measurements - the above measurements are restricted to those envolving mean and standard deviation.
